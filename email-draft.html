MTR Direct

"From inquiry to move-in. Everything an MTR host needs to fill their property — without the platform fees."

The Problem

Medium-term rental hosts — traveling nurse properties, corporate housing, relocation rentals — are stuck between two bad options:

Option 1: Airbnb/VRBO — handles the full workflow but takes 15–20% in fees, controls guest relationships, and optimizes for short stays over 30+ day bookings.

Option 2: Furnished Finder — excellent lead generation for MTR specifically, but the post-inquiry process is painful. Hosts must purchase lease templates separately, cobble together background check services, handle payments manually, and stitch together 4–5 different tools to close a single tenant.

The gap: No one owns the full MTR host workflow. Every existing tool — Lodgify, Hospitable, Guesty — was built for short-term rentals. MTR has fundamentally different needs: monthly billing, lease agreements, tenant screening, and 30+ day legal compliance.

The Solution

MTR Direct is an all-in-one platform for MTR hosts to attract, screen, and close tenants — without platform fees or operational headaches.

  • Corporate housing providers — 1–10 property portfolios

Competitive Landscape

Two categories of adjacent tools exist, but neither serves MTR:

  • STR tools (Lodgify, Hospitable, Guesty) — booking sites but no leases, monthly billing, or tenant screening
  • Long-term rental tools (Avail, TurboTenant) — screening and leases but no booking sites or MTR-specific workflows

MTR Direct bridges both — the booking site of an STR tool with the operational backend of a long-term rental tool, purpose-built for 30–180 day stays.

Defensibility

  1. First-mover advantage in MTR-specific tooling — own the SEO and category
  2. Network effects — verified guest directory grows with every host
  3. Data moat — aggregate MTR pricing and occupancy data across markets
  4. Workflow lock-in — leases, payment history, and guest records live in the platform
  5. Community — MTR host community with playbooks, templates, and shared knowledge

Go-to-Market

Phase 1 — Prove It (Now → Month 3): Build and operate our own direct booking site. Get real bookings. Document the process, savings, and results as content.

Phase 2 — First 10 Customers (Month 3–6): Offer free/discounted builds in Furnished Finder Facebook groups and traveling nurse communities. Publish the "MTR Direct Booking Playbook" for email capture.

Phase 3 — Scale (Month 6–12): Launch self-serve template builder. Add screening and lease features. SEO and content marketing. Explore partnerships with Furnished Finder, MTR coaches, and nurse staffing agencies.

Phase 4 — Platform (Month 12+): Guest directory, pricing intelligence, multi-property dashboard, and API integrations.
