# MTR Direct ‚Äî Business Plan
*"Airbnb's automation without Airbnb's fees. From ad to move-in, fully automated."*

## The Problem

Medium-term rental (MTR) hosts ‚Äî traveling nurse properties, corporate housing, relocation rentals ‚Äî are stuck between two bad options:

1. **Airbnb/VRBO** ‚Äî handles everything but takes 15-20%, controls your guests, and optimizes for short stays
2. **Furnished Finder** ‚Äî great lead gen for MTR specifically, but the post-inquiry process is painful: hosts buy lease templates separately, cobble together background checks, handle payments manually, and piece together 4-5 tools to close a tenant

**Nobody owns the full MTR host workflow.** Every existing tool (Lodgify, Hospitable, Guesty) is built for short-term rentals. MTR has fundamentally different needs: monthly billing, lease agreements, tenant screening, 30+ day legal requirements.

## The Solution

**MTR Direct** ‚Äî an all-in-one platform for MTR hosts to attract, screen, and close tenants without platform fees.

### The Stack

| Layer | What It Does | Replaces |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Direct Booking Site** | Beautiful, SEO-optimized property page with inquiry form | Carrd + manual website |
| **Lead Management** | Inquiry dashboard, auto-responses, availability calendar | Email + spreadsheets |
| **Tenant Screening** | One-click credit + background checks | Separate services ($30-50 each) |
| **Lease Generation** | MTR-specific templates, auto-filled, e-signed | Buying templates ($50-200) |
| **Payments** | Monthly invoicing, auto-charge, security deposit handling | Venmo/Zelle/manual |
| **Move-in Automation** | Welcome guide, smart lock codes, utility info, house manual | Text messages + PDFs |
| **Marketing Engine** | Auto-generated Facebook/Instagram ads targeting nurses + relocators | Hosts doing nothing / hiring agencies ($1K+/mo) |

### Why This Wins

- **Template gets them in the door** (low friction, immediate value)
- **Operational backend keeps them** (high switching cost ‚Äî their leases, payment history, guest data all live here)
- **Network effects over time** ‚Äî verified guest directory, pricing data across markets

### Additional Revenue
- **Ad spend margin** ‚Äî 15-20% of host's ad budget (host spends $300/mo on ads, we keep $45-60)
- **Tenant screening fees** ‚Äî pass-through + small markup ($5-10/check)
- **Payment processing** ‚Äî standard 2.9% + $0.30 (Stripe, keep the margin)
- **One-time setup fee** ‚Äî $97-197 for site build + onboarding

### The ROI Pitch
For a $4,500/mo rental, Airbnb takes ~$675-900/mo in fees. MTR Direct charges $149/mo. **Host saves $500+/mo** while getting the same hands-off automation. Pays for itself 3-4x over.

### The Full Automation Flow
1. Host signs up ‚Üí answers 10 questions, uploads photos
2. AI auto-generates site (copy, SEO, template selection)
3. Ads auto-launch on Facebook/Instagram (targeting nurses + relocators in their market)
4. Lead comes in ‚Üí auto-response with availability + "ready to apply?" link
5. Guest applies ‚Üí screening runs automatically, host gets ‚úÖ or ‚ùå
6. Lease auto-generates ‚Üí pre-filled, sent for e-signature
7. Payment auto-starts ‚Üí first month + deposit via Stripe, recurring billing
8. Move-in packet auto-sends ‚Üí lock code, welcome guide, WiFi, local recs

- **Ads:** Facebook Marketing API
