## Secondary Segments

### 1. Corporate Relocators (20% of TAM)

**Who:** Employees moving for new jobs, waiting for permanent housing

**Contract length:** 1-6 months

**Budget:** Company-paid, typically $2,000-4,000/month

**Where to find them:**
- Corporate housing platforms (CHBO)
- Relocation companies
- HR departments of large employers

**Pain points:**
- Need move-in ready, furnished
- Professional environment
- Lease flexibility
- Near office/downtown

### 2. Students & Interns (10% of TAM)

**Who:** Graduate students, medical residents, summer interns

**Contract length:** 3-12 months

**Budget:** Personal or stipend, typically $1,000-2,000/month

**Where to find them:**
- University housing offices
- Medical school networks
- Internship programs

**Pain points:**
- Affordable furnished options
- Near campus or hospital
- Flexible lease end dates

### 3. Insurance Displacement (5% of TAM)

**Who:** Families displaced by fire, flood, or home repairs

**Contract length:** 1-6 months (highly variable)

**Budget:** Insurance-paid, typically market rate

**Where to find them:**
- Insurance adjusters
- Restoration companies
- Property managers

**Pain points:**
- Need NOW (emergency)
- Family-friendly
- Pet-friendly
- Near original location

### 4. Digital Nomads / Remote Workers (5% of TAM)

**Who:** Location-independent workers exploring cities

**Contract length:** 1-3 months

**Budget:** Self-paid, $1,500-3,000/month

**Where to find them:**
- Nomad Facebook groups
- Nomad List
- Twitter / X
- Coworking spaces

**Pain points:**
- Good WiFi essential
- Workspace in unit
- Walkable neighborhoods
- Flexibility to extend
