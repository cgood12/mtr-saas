# MTR Direct — Marketing Strategy for The Pearl Craftsman
*Live experiment: prove the model before scaling it*

## The Goal
Drive direct bookings to mtr-direct-booking.vercel.app. Measure everything. Use learnings to build the playbook we sell to other hosts.

## Target Guests (San Antonio)
1. **Travel nurses** — Closest hospitals: Baptist Medical, Methodist, University Health, South Texas Medical Center
2. **Military** — Lackland AFB, Fort Sam Houston, Randolph AFB (PCS moves, TDY, training)
3. **Relocating professionals** — SA is a growing tech/business hub
4. **Medical travelers** — Visiting family in hospital, extended stays

## Channel Playbook

### Tier 1: Free/Low Cost (Do First)
**Facebook Groups** — highest ROI, zero cost
- "San Antonio Travel Nurse Housing"
- "San Antonio Furnished Rentals"
- "Military Housing San Antonio TX"
- "Fort Sam Houston Housing"
- "Lackland AFB Housing"
- Post: photo of property + "Available [dates] — 3BR furnished home in Tobin Hill. Direct booking saves you 15% vs Airbnb. [link]"
- Do this weekly. Rotate photos.

**Furnished Finder optimization**
- Response time is a ranking factor — Alex now responds in <10 min (done)
- Add direct booking site link to FF profile description
- Update photos, amenities, highlight hospitals/bases proximity

**CHBO (Corporate Housing by Owner)**
- corporatehousingbyowner.com — annual fee ~$599, targets corporate renters
- High quality leads, less competition than FF

**Homads**
- Free to list (3% booking fee) — targets relocating families
- Good fit for SA market

**Travel nurse agency outreach**
- Email housing coordinators at: Aya Healthcare, AMN Healthcare, Cross Country, Travel Nurse Across America
- "We have furnished 3BR near Baptist Medical, available [dates], direct booking available"
- These agencies place 100s of nurses in SA — one relationship = recurring leads

### Tier 2: Paid (Once We Have Analytics)
**Google Ads**
- Keywords: "furnished rental san antonio", "travel nurse housing san antonio", "monthly rental san antonio tx"
- Budget: $15-20/day to start, pause if no conversions after 2 weeks
- Landing page: mtr-direct-booking.vercel.app

**Facebook/Instagram Ads**
- Target: nurses, healthcare workers, military in San Antonio radius
- Creative: before/after (Airbnb fees vs direct savings), property photos
- Budget: $10/day

### Tier 3: Content (Builds Long-Term)
- "How to find furnished housing as a travel nurse in San Antonio" — SEO blog post
- List on Zillow as furnished monthly rental
- Reddit: r/travelnursing, r/solotravel — answer housing questions, mention property

## Tracking
Once GA4 is live on the site:
- Source/medium for every inquiry
- Conversion rate: visits → inquiries → bookings
- Cost per lead by channel

## What Success Looks Like
- 1 direct booking in 30 days = proof of concept
- Track: what channel drove it, what was the inquiry-to-booking timeline, what would have been lost to Airbnb commission

## Timeline
- Week 1: Post in Facebook groups, optimize FF listing, get GA4 live
- Week 2: Agency outreach, add CHBO/Homads listings
- Week 3: Review analytics, decide on paid spend
- Week 4: First booking report
